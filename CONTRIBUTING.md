# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in contributing.

Before a pull request can be merged, you must sign a Contributor License
Agreement (CLA) granting Rockbound Capital the right to license your
contribution under other terms, including commercial licenses. This keeps
dual licensing of the project possible. Open an issue or email
monte@rockbound-capital.com to receive the CLA.

By submitting a contribution you confirm that you have the right to license
the contributed code and that it contains no third-party code under
incompatible terms.
